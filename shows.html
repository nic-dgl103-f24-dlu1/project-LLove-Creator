Up Next: Member's Show - "Joy"

Pearl Ellis Gallery | Nov 26 - dec 21, 2024

Celebrate the spirit of creativity and happiness at the Pearl Ellis Gallery's annual Members Show, "Joy." Running from November 26 to December 21, this non-juried exhibit showcases the diverse interpretations of joy by local artists. From uplifting landscapes to vibrant abstracts, the works capture moments of cheer and festive spirit, offering a variety of styles and mediums.

Join us for the opening reception on Saturday, Nov 30, from 1 to 3 p.m. to meet the artists and enjoy light refreshments. Admission is free!
